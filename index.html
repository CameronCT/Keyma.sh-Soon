Frequently Asked Questions
Can we join as a Tester?
We are currently looking for testers. In order to apply as a tester, you must fill out the form located in #tester-applications. Keep in mind if caught posting more than one application, you will automatically be denied and kicked from the Discord.
When will this be released?
As of right now we currently do not have a specific release date in mind though it will be in 2021, we want to release a product that we can be extremely proud of. Make sure to keep up to date in our #announcements channel regarding developments on the game!
Will Keyma.sh be Free to Play?
Yes. However we will be placing ads tastefully around the site where we can still keep afloat but not interfere with the gaming experience. Alternatively we hope to release a yearly fee to permanently get rid of advertisements.
I work for a School, how can we use Keyma.sh with our students?
We offer an intuitive lobby experience that allows students and teachers to easily connect with each other privately.
